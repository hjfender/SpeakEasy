How It Works

Meet New People:

What are you interested in? We'll set you up with someone in your community and help you get a conversation rolling on topics you choose.

Relax!

All conversations begin anonymously to take off some of the pressure. But when you hit it off, you and your new bestie can reveal identites and grab a cup of coffee together offline.

Never a Lull:

Even when conversation slows, there's always something to do. Get to know your new friend by playing a game or taking a Buzzfeed quiz together.
